What is it?

Roboguide is an “assistant” that consists of displaying information about what the user selects on the device (information about tourist places, typical dishes, cultural information about the place where they are), it is also adapted to carry out the currency exchange according to the user requires it, reducing the use of printed media, providing information in an interactive way and facilitating access for people with disabilities.
