How did it emerge?

Roboguide arises when we are assigned a project for academic development, the idea of ​​Roboguide arises with the need to have information about the place where you are, it is focused and directed mainly at people who travel and who have a motor disability, Roboguide will be available at airports and will be free for anyone interested in learning more about the place they visit.
